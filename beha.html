Behavioral Diagrams

Activity Diagram

The Black circle is the starting point or the "Teacher", the teacher give an assignment to the students. The student reviewed the assignment and after they review it they are going
to answer it. The diamond represents a decision symbol, while they're answering their assignments, the easy one will be done first and the hard will remain pending. They have 2 decision to make when answering their assignment, its either they will give up or they will continue so they can submit their assignment. After the assignment is completed, they'll pass it when the deadline comes the grade work will now start and after the gradework sheet, the teacher will distribute their assignment and their assignment is now graded. The students who did their homework got the grades, the Circle symbol with another circle inside of it represents the end point symbol.

Use Case Diagram

A man with a chainsaw interacts with the environment around him. Depending on the situation and the context of the situation, he might fall into one of many different use cases. Does he seem to be on his way to work? Is there anything ominous about the way he is wielding his chainsaw? For example, if he is using the chainsaw in a non-occupational setting, we might have reason to think that he falls within the scope of "scary."

Communication Diagram

The customer wants find a product in Online shopping, He is going to search what's product he want to buy. After searching for the product, he's going to look at the product and he is going to decide whether he wanted to buy it or just add it to his cart. If he is going to buy the product he will check it out and if he's just looking for something even better, he'll add it to cart. If he check it out he will proceed to the payment method where he needs to choose what method of payments he wants to. There's COD(Cash on delivery), Gcash, and Paymaya, after selecting what payment method he's now ready to click sa place order and wait for the item to arrive.

sequence Diagram

We have Atm and bank here, this is how the atm works in sequence diagram. First, you need to transfer your funds into the bank and the bank will reply(the arrow with dash line represent a reply or message) to get your account and you need to log in you bank account. After you log in your account, they're going to ask you how much or the amount of money you want to transfer. The box is called the alternative symbol, while performing your transaction there's 2 possible outcomes for the action you did and its success and failure.
